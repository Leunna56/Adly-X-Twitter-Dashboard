    📊 How This Dashboard Gets Its Data

    All post-level activity is logged manually by the team in the Adly X Twitter Project Google Sheet. This dashboard fetches live data directly from that sheet — hit Refresh Data anytime new posts have been logged.

    📚 Terms & Definitions

    Impressions

    How many times a post was displayed in someone’s feed. Pure reach — even if they just scrolled past it, it counts.

    Engagements

    X’s pre-computed total of all interactions on a post: likes + replies + reposts + clicks + bookmarks + profile visits + detail expands + URL clicks. Any one of those counts.

    Engagement Rate (ER)

    Engagements ÷ Impressions × 100. Shows what percentage of viewers actually did something after seeing the post. Higher is better.

    Posts

    Original content published by a team member — not in response to anyone else’s tweet.

    Replies

    Responses to other people’s posts or threads. A core warm-up strategy for new X accounts — builds visibility by joining existing conversations.

    Actions (Total)

    The total count of posts + replies logged by the team in a given period. The main activity volume metric.

    Most Engagements (Top 3)

    Posts ranked by raw engagement count. Shows which content got the most total interactions, regardless of reach.

    Highest ER (Top 3)

    Posts ranked by engagement rate, limited to posts with 10+ impressions. Shows which content was most efficient at converting viewers into engagers.
